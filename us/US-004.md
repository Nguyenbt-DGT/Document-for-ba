## User Story

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **User story** | **As** a Manulife agent (FA) wanting to leave a professional document with my customer after a consultation, **I want** M-Smart to generate one illustration PDF with a clear infographic layout — showing each family member's benefits visually, with a coverage-timeline chart, a family protection map, and a fund allocation chart — instead of a plain numeric table, **So that** the customer can understand the document immediately, and I have a stronger pitching tool that increases my close rate. |
| **Actor:** | Manulife Agent (FA) |
| **Trigger:** | A **Priced, budget-fit Package** is available from US-003 (Budget Allocation & Premium Calculation) |
| **Pre-condition:** | US-003 has produced a complete priced package (per-member products/riders + premiums + budget-fit status) for MI and up to 3 OI |
| **Post-condition:** | A downloadable PDF illustration document covering the entire family in a single file is available to the FA |

1. **Flow:** N/A — conversational trigger ("Xem minh họa PDF") followed by a downloadable file; see
   Acceptance Criteria for exact content requirements.
2. **Screen demo:** N/A — output is a PDF document; structure is fully specified by the 7-section table and

## 1. Scope

- **In scope:**
  - A single, unified PDF covering the whole family (PO/MI + up to 3 OI) in one document.
  - 7 fixed sections: Cover Page, Family Coverage Map, Premium Overview, Per-Member Detail Cards, Premium
    Summary Table, Policy Value Chart (ILP only), Legal + Agent Contact.
  - Per-member accent-color visual system, greyscale-print-safe.
  - Bilingual (Vietnamese primary, English technical terms in parentheses) content with correct UTF-8
    rendering.
  - Generation performance SLA and output file-quality constraints.
  - Compliance-reviewed template content (no PII beyond role/age/gender).
- **Out of scope:**
  - Family intake, recommendation, or premium calculation logic — belongs to **US-001/US-002/US-003**;
    this story only renders their already-finalized output.
  - Electronic signature (eSignature) on the PDF.
  - Saving PDF history to the customer's CRM profile.
  - Automatic delivery of the PDF via email/Zalo.
  - Per-FA template customization.

## 2. Requirement

#### R.01 Cover Page

Manulife Vietnam logo, title *"Phiếu Minh Họa Gói Bảo Vệ Gia Đình"*, the PO's role/age/gender (never a
real name), the FA's name and agent code (from MPA), the generation date, and the mandatory disclaimer
*"Đây là tài liệu tham khảo, chưa có giá trị pháp lý"* — with no personally identifying information beyond
role and age, per InfoSec policy MGAFS-1283 and Vietnamese insurance regulation.

#### R.02 Family Coverage Map (Hub & Spoke)

An inline SVG infographic with MI at the center (hub) showing the base product and total sum assured, each
OI as a spoke around it showing their applicable riders and sum assured, and PO shown separately (with
role + total APE) when PO ≠ MI. Each member has an avatar icon (male/female/child), role label, and age.
Uses the fixed per-member accent-color palette, renders correctly for N = 1, 2, or 3 OI, and remains
legible in greyscale (black-and-white) printing.

#### R.03 Premium Overview (Donut Chart + Dual Timeline Bar)

A donut chart showing premium allocation by member (percentage + VND), capped at 5 segments with no slice
smaller than 5% of the total, with the total monthly premium prominently displayed. A dual timeline bar
shows the payment term (solid bar, N years) against the protection term (extended bar, M years, M > N),
annotated directly on the bar (not relying on color alone) to make the "pay for N years, protected for M
years" distinction unmistakable.

#### R.04 Per-Member Detail Cards

One card per insured (MI + each OI) with a header (avatar icon, role, age, product name) and their riders
rendered as benefit chips (icon + benefit name + sum assured + annual premium). No card shows an empty
rider list for a member with no riders; the MI's value table shows at least 5 rows.

#### R.05 Premium Summary Table

A table with columns Member | Role | Product | Annual Premium | Monthly Premium, a subtotal per member,
a bolded, highlighted grand total, Vietnamese number formatting, a budget-comparison column ("Trong ngân
sách ✅" / "Vượt ngân sách ⚠️"), and the mandatory footnote *"Đây là bản tóm tắt minh hoạ. Quyền lợi thực
tế theo hợp đồng bảo hiểm."*

#### R.06 Policy Value Timeline Chart (ILP only)

A line chart (X = Policy Year ascending, Y = VND value) with two lines — high and low projected values —
showing at minimum PY 1, 5, 10, 15, 20, and the maturity year, with the final premium-payment year and
maturity year annotated as milestones. The high/low lines must never swap position, and policy years must
never render out of ascending order.

#### R.07 Fund Allocation Chart (ILP only)

For an ILP base product, a donut/pie chart with one slice per fund (name + percentage, summing to exactly
100%), consistent with what was already shown in the conversational premium display (US-003). This entire
section is omitted for a non-ILP (e.g. ULP) base product.

#### R.08 Bilingual Terminology & Font Rendering

Primary body text in Vietnamese; technical terms shown bilingually in parentheses (e.g. *"Bên Mua Bảo
Hiểm (BMBH)"*, *"ILP26 — Sản phẩm liên kết đầu tư"*); full Vietnamese diacritic support via UTF-8, using
Be Vietnam Pro (or the Manulife JH Sans Optimized fallback) as the rendering font.

#### R.09 Generation SLA & Reliability

Generate and make the PDF downloadable within 30 seconds for up to 4 insured, with a visible progress
indicator while generating, and a friendly retry option if generation exceeds the SLA.

#### R.10 PDF Output Quality

Resolution ≥ 150 DPI (no pixelated charts), file size ≤ 5MB, printable on A4 without content clipping, and
a header (Manulife logo + "PHIẾU MINH HỌA — BẢO MẬT") and footer (page X/N, generation date, agent code)
on every page.

## 3. Business Rules & Validations

| Rule ID | Rule Name | Business Description | Impacted flow | Example Scenario |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| BR-US004-01 | No PII beyond role/age/gender | The cover page and every section must never print a real customer name. Refer to AC1 | create (PDF) | Cover page shows "Bên Mua Bảo Hiểm: Nam, 34 tuổi" — never "Nguyễn Văn A" |
| BR-US004-02 | Fixed per-member accent-color palette | MI = #00A758, BMBH = #1A6FA8, Spouse = #D77D28, Child 1 = #7B5EA7, Child 2 = #E05C73; must remain distinguishable in greyscale. Refer to AC2 | create (PDF) | A black-and-white printout still visually separates each member's section |
| BR-US004-03 | Donut chart segment limits | At most 5 segments; no segment may represent less than 5% of the total. Refer to AC3 | create (PDF) | A rider contributing under 5% of total premium is grouped rather than shown as its own sliver |
| BR-US004-04 | No empty rider sections | A member with zero riders never shows an empty benefit-chip block (per MGAFS-1781, consistent with US-003). Refer to AC4 | create (PDF) | MI-only package with no OI shows no blank OI cards |
| BR-US004-05 | Timeline chart ordering integrity | High/low value lines must never swap, and Policy Years must render in strict ascending order (per MGAFS-1953/1855/1978). Refer to AC6 | create (PDF) | PY11 must never appear positioned after PY20 on the chart |
| BR-US004-06 | Fund allocation chart is ILP-only | The fund allocation section is included only when the base product is ILP, and is fully omitted otherwise. Refer to AC7 | create (PDF) | A term-life (non-ILP) base product produces a PDF with no fund allocation page |
| BR-US004-07 | Vietnamese number formatting | All monetary values use a period as the thousands separator (per MGAFS-2050, consistent with US-003). Refer to AC5 | create (PDF) | "59.900.000 đ" not "59,900,000 đ" |
| BR-US004-08 | Output quality floor | Every generated PDF must meet ≥150 DPI, ≤5MB, and A4-printable constraints before being returned to the FA. Refer to AC11 | create (PDF) | A PDF exceeding 5MB is rejected/re-optimized before delivery, not sent as-is |

## 4. Impact and Risk

1. **Backend:** a multi-insured PDF layout/rendering pipeline (server-side SVG/Canvas, Puppeteer-safe)
   consuming the Priced Package from US-003; extends the existing single-insured PDF generation engine
   (`gen-pdf-engine` / `genPDF_by_fo`) rather than replacing it.
2. **Compliance risk — the longest lead-time item in the whole epic:** the new infographic template
   requires Compliance sign-off before it can go live; this should start **in parallel with US-001/US-002**
   rather than waiting until this story's sprint, to avoid discovering a layout constraint late that forces
   a redesign.
3. **Regression risk:** the existing single-persona PDF generation path must continue to work unchanged.
4. **Known-defect risk:** timeline-chart ordering bugs (MGAFS-1953/1855/1978) already exist in the
   single-person path and must not be reintroduced or amplified by the multi-member layout.

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

1. **Performance:** PDF generation and download-readiness within ≤ 30 seconds for up to 4 insured, with a
   progress indicator and a retry option on timeout.
2. **Output quality:** ≥ 150 DPI, ≤ 5MB file size, A4-printable, correct headers/footers on every page.
3. **Localization:** Vietnamese-primary bilingual content, UTF-8/Be Vietnam Pro font rendering with full
   diacritic support.
4. **Accessibility/print-safety:** greyscale-safe color usage — no information conveyed by color alone.

## 6. Dependencies / References

1. **US-002 (Product & Rider Recommendation)** and **US-003 (Budget Allocation & Premium Calculation)** —
   upstream; this story renders their finalized output and must not re-derive business logic.
2. PDF generation engine (`gen-pdf-engine` / `genPDF_by_fo`) — BE/DevOps Team.
3. Server-side SVG/Canvas chart rendering library (Puppeteer-safe) — BE Team.
4. Compliance sign-off for the new infographic PDF template — PO + Compliance (start early, see Impact #2).
5. Vietnamese font package (Be Vietnam Pro / UTF-8) installed on the PDF generation server — DevOps.
6. Design spec: `ai-works/MSmart/docs/DESIGN-family-quick-pitch.md` — DESGOD → fegod design team.

## 7. Technical Note

1. This story must treat the Priced Package (from US-003) as read-only input — no premium recalculation or
   product re-selection logic belongs here.
2. Per-member accent colors (BR-US004-02) and chart data (donut/timeline/fund allocation) must stay
   numerically consistent with what was already shown to the FA conversationally in US-003/US-002 — the
   PDF must never show different numbers than the chat.
3. Reuse the existing single-persona PDF header/footer/compliance boilerplate; only the body sections
   (Coverage Map, per-member cards, multi-member summary table) are new.

## 8. Acceptance Criteria

#### AC1. [Happy Path] Cover page renders with correct content and no PII

**Given** US-003 has produced a complete priced package and the FA requests the illustration PDF  
**When** PDF generation runs  
**Then** the cover page includes the Manulife Vietnam logo, the title, the PO's role/age/gender (no real name), the FA's name and agent code, the generation date, and the required disclaimer  
**And** no personally identifying information beyond role and age appears anywhere on the cover.

#### AC2. [Happy Path] Family Coverage Map renders correctly for 1, 2, or 3 OI

**Given** a priced package with MI + N OI (N = 1, 2, or 3)  
**When** the Family Coverage Map section is generated  
**Then** MI appears at the center with base product and total sum assured, each OI appears as a spoke with their riders and sum assured, and PO is shown separately (role + total APE) if PO ≠ MI  
**And** the layout adjusts correctly for each value of N, uses the fixed per-member accent-color palette, and remains legible when printed in greyscale.

#### AC3. [Happy Path] Premium Overview chart and timeline bar render per spec

**Given** a priced package with per-member premium data  
**When** the Premium Overview section is generated  
**Then** the donut chart shows premium allocation per member (≤5 segments, no segment under 5%) with the total monthly premium prominent, and the dual timeline bar clearly annotates payment term vs. protection term directly on the bar.

#### AC4. [Happy Path] Per-member detail cards show benefit chips with no empty sections

**Given** each insured's finalized rider list from US-003  
**When** the Per-Member Detail Cards section is generated  
**Then** every insured gets a header card (avatar, role, age, product) and their riders as benefit chips  
**And** no card renders an empty rider list for a member with no riders, and the MI's value table shows at least 5 rows.

#### AC5. [Happy Path] Premium Summary Table shows correct totals and budget status

**Given** the finalized priced package  
**When** the Premium Summary Table section is generated  
**Then** it shows Member | Role | Product | Annual | Monthly columns, a subtotal per member, a bolded grand total, the budget-fit status ("Trong ngân sách ✅" / "Vượt ngân sách ⚠️"), Vietnamese number formatting, and the mandatory footnote disclaimer.

#### AC6. [Validation] Timeline chart preserves strict Policy Year order and value integrity

**Given** the illustration table data from the premium calculation response (PY 1 → maturity)  
**When** the Policy Value Chart is rendered (ILP base product only)  
**Then** Policy Years render in strictly ascending order with at least PY 1, 5, 10, 15, 20 and the maturity year shown, with the final payment year and maturity year annotated  
**And** the high and low value lines never swap position at any point on the chart.

#### AC7. [Edge Case] Fund allocation chart shown only for ILP and always sums to 100%

**Given** the base product is ILP  
**When** the Fund Allocation Chart section is generated  
**Then** each fund appears as one slice with a name and percentage, the percentages sum to exactly 100%, and the values match what was already shown in the conversational premium display  
**And** if the base product is not ILP (e.g. ULP), this entire section is omitted from the PDF, not shown empty or with placeholder data.

#### AC8. [Validation] Bilingual content renders correctly with full Vietnamese diacritics

**Given** the finalized PDF content  
**When** the document is rendered  
**Then** all body text is Vietnamese, technical terms show the English equivalent in parentheses, and every Vietnamese diacritical mark renders correctly with no font/encoding errors (UTF-8, Be Vietnam Pro or the approved fallback).

#### AC9. [Negative Case] No compliance-approved template exists for the requested combination

**Given** the finalized priced package uses a product/rider/member-count combination that has no compliance-approved template variant in the design spec (e.g. a base product not yet covered by the approved infographic layout)  
**When** the FA requests the illustration PDF  
**Then** M-Smart does **not** fall back to an unapproved or improvised layout — it blocks generation and tells the FA this combination is not yet supported, rather than silently producing a non-compliant document.

#### AC10. [Happy Path] Generation completes within the SLA with a progress indicator

**Given** the FA requests the illustration PDF for a family of up to 4 insured  
**When** generation begins  
**Then** a progress indicator is shown (*"⏳ Đang tạo phiếu minh họa... (thường mất 15–30 giây)"*) and the completed, downloadable PDF is ready within 30 seconds.

#### AC11. [Exception Case] Generation exceeds the SLA or fails

**Given** PDF generation is in progress  
**When** it exceeds 30 seconds or otherwise fails  
**Then** M-Smart shows a friendly error message with a "Thử lại" (retry) option  
**And** it does not return a partial, corrupted, or silently-incomplete PDF to the FA.

#### AC12. [Validation] Output file meets quality and print constraints

**Given** any successfully generated illustration PDF  
**When** the file is produced  
**Then** its resolution is ≥ 150 DPI, its file size is ≤ 5MB, it prints correctly on A4 without clipped content, and every page carries the required header and footer.

#### AC13. [Integration Failure] Upstream priced-package data is incomplete or inconsistent

**Given** the data handed off from US-003 is missing a required field (e.g. a member's premium was never calculated) or is internally inconsistent (e.g. budget-fit status missing)  
**When** PDF generation is attempted  
**Then** M-Smart does **not** render a PDF with blank, placeholder, or fabricated values for the missing data — it blocks generation and returns a clear message to the FA that the package must be finalized in the chat first, before a PDF can be produced.
